## Being Consistent

The key to branding is consistency. People need to be able to set expectations.

For every video you do, ask yourself these questions:

> _Who are you?_
>
> _What is your message?_
>
> _What value are you providing?_
>
> _Who is it for?_

The answers to these questions make up your brand. They may change gradually over time ‚Äì i.e. if you start making videos as a 16 year old, for other 16 year olds, then the ‚ÄòWho is it for?‚Äô will change as you and your subscriber base get older ‚Äì but in general the answers should always be the same.
